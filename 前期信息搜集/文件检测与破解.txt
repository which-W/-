如果是7z文件

7z x backup.7z 解压缩
如果加密可以尝试暴力破解
使用7z2john转为hash
7z2john backup.7z > back7z_hash
john --format=7z --wordlist=/usr/share/wordlists/rockyou.txt back7z_hash

如果获取ssh私钥文件但私钥已经被加密可以尝试暴力破解
sshjohn id_rsa > id_rsa_hash
john --wordlist=/usr/share/wordlists/rockyou.txt id_rsa_hash(让其自己判断是那种ssh类型)
